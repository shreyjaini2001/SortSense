# Privacy Policy

SortSense collects **no personally identifiable information (PII)** at any point.

- Webcam frames are processed in memory and immediately discarded — never stored to disk.
- The triage log stores only: timestamp, item category, bin decision, confidence score, and signal breakdown. No images. No personal data.
- No user accounts, login, or tracking.
- GDPR and COPPA compliant by design.
